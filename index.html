Who We Are

About Azalor Technologies

A studio built on the belief that great engineering starts with deep understanding.

Azalor Technologies is a research-driven engineering studio founded on the principle that the best software systems are born from rigorous investigation and thoughtful design — not shortcuts.

We specialize in real-time systems, data pipelines at scale, and AI-powered platforms. From building streaming microservices processing millions of events daily, to architecting intelligent contact center solutions — we engineer systems that perform under pressure.

Our work sits at the intersection of AI, distributed computing, and scalable architecture. We focus on building technology that is honest, responsible, and genuinely beneficial — engineering that serves a purpose beyond the code itself.

Every project at Azalor Technologies is treated as a research endeavor — grounded in evidence, guided by principles, and executed with engineering excellence.
